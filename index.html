About

Hi, I'm Henrik Karlsson — A Creative Designer based in Sarasota, Florida. Specializing in Motion Design, Photography, and Web Design.

My passion for design began at a young age and has grown into a professional career spanning over 20 years. Throughout this journey, I've honed a wide range of creative and technical skills. Clean, functional, and minimal design is at the core of who I am and is reflected in everything I produce. I genuinely appreciate and enjoy the art of visual communication.

In my spare time, I enjoy an active, outdoor lifestyle. Soccer has been a major part of my life since childhood, and today I support my two boys in their own soccer journeys. I also have a strong passion for photography, often capturing the moments that inspire me.
